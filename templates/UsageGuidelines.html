Usage Guidelines

Last updated on 15th Dec 2022

The following guidelines are examples of what we deem as unsafe content to utilize our platform. These examples are to show the spectrum of unsafe content that have been used as inputs into our platform. Ultimately, the inputs are the responsibility of the user before our technology generates content based off of the original inputs.

TestEra strongly forbids the usage of its platform for any of the following purposes. These examples are illustrative but not exhaustive; TestEra reserves the right to terminate accounts for harms which are not listed at its sole discretion.

Disallowed Use Cases

Harassment - Writing content or messages intended to provoke, intimidate or harass others is prohibited. Users who violate these guidelines will be blocked or banned.

Hate speech - Creating content promoting violence or discrimination against a person or a group of people.

Promoting self-harm - Creating content regarding self-harm or with the intention to encourage others to do so.

Spam - Creating unsolicited messages, content or information to large groups of people.

Fraud - Creating misleading or false information or documents to defraud others.

Impersonation - Creating content that misrepresents the source or identity of the content creator.

Misinformation - Creating false, inaccurate or misleading information or to manipulate public opinion.

Political Content - We have zero tolerance for content that can be defined as political. This includes telling users to vote in a specific manner; posts about political figures, legislation, political parties, political protests, etc.

Social Media with the intent to sow discourse - Creating content on social media with the intent to create division within households or communities.

Illegal content - Generating any content that is related to any illegal industry including but not limited to prostitution, illicit drugs, child pornography, etc.

Gambling - Using our services for gambling-related purposes including advertising or promoting it, among other unregulated activities.

Porn/Adult Content - Using our services for pornographic services and content.

Monitoring

We use OpenAI's content filter to check for violations on both inputs and outputs.
